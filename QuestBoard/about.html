About

Inspired by the Hunter's Guild theme seen in such games as Monster Hunter and other RPGs, QuestBoard is an interactive experience between the Twitch communityand their favorite streamer. The community can set up challenges in the form of a "quest" that the streamer can choose to take. If the streamer completes the quest they will get the quest rewards attached to the quest. The quest reward is a donation amount that the quest creator attaches to the quest. If the streamer fails to complete all the quest victory conditions, or manages to meet one of the failure conditions they lose the reward and the quest reward goes towards charity.

How it works

  1. Create a quest
  2. Establish the Win / Failure Conditions
  3. Set the quest reward amount and choose a charity
  4. Submit

Terms and Conditions

Because of the open nature of QuestBoard there are some guidelines when creating quests.

QuestBoard reserves the right to cancel quests deemed to be inappropriate (i.e. Racist/ Sexist/ etc.). Quest rewards on canceled quests are donated to charity and no refunds will be issued.
