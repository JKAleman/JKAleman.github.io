General Campus Information


One of the four main ASU locations found in Arizona, the Polytechinc Campus was originally founded in the Fall of 1996. Constructed on land previously occupied by Williams Air Force Base, the campus would open its doors that same year. Today, the Polytechinc Campus is home to over 8,000 students in various technology-related fields. Students within the many different programs offered at the Polytechinc School are actively engaged in their fields of study, with research software development, and more all taking place within on-campus faciilies.
